What are Criminal Records Expungement?

Expungement is a court-ordered process in which legal record of an arrest or a criminal conviction is "sealed" or erased in the eyes of the law.

When a conviction is expunged, The process may also be reffered to as "Setting aside a criminal conviction."

What do bitcoin miners do?

The role of miners is to secure the network and to process every Bitcoin transaction.

Miners achieve this by solving computational problems which allows them to chain together blocks of transactions (hence Bitcoin's famous "blockchain").

Can credit card debt result in jail sentence?

Some states-roughly a third-still use jail as a method to coerce debtors to pay certain debts.

Today, you cannot go to prison for failing to pay for a "civil debt" like a credit card, loan, or hospital bill. You can however, be forced to go to jail if you don't pay your taxes or child support.
